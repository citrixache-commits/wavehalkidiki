OUR STORY

A taverna on the
Ouranoupoli shore

where the day begins with the waves

Steps from the beach in Ouranoupoli — the last village before Mount Athos — Waves serves the Halkidiki day in full: sunrise coffee and warm pastries, long seaside lunches of grilled fish and octopus, and slow evenings of mixed grills, crepes and cocktails under the wicker lamps.

GOOD TO KNOW

Before you sail to Athos

questions pilgrims ask us
What time do you open before the boat to Mount Athos?

We open at 05:30, every single day — coffee, warm pastries and a full breakfast before the first ferry leaves Ouranoupoli.

Do you serve fasting (lenten) dishes?

Yes. Alongside fresh seafood, the menu includes lenten options — fasting pizza, vegetable soups and salads. Ask us for today's fasting dishes.

Where exactly are you in Ouranoupoli?

Right on the beach, a short walk from the port where the boats leave for Mount Athos. Look for the Waves sign on the seafront.

Can we come as a group before the pilgrimage?

Of course — call +30 2377 071088 and the table will be ready before your boat departs.
